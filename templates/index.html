    Frequenty Asked Questions

    • What types of materials do you use for window screen repairs?

      We utilize a range of high-quality materials to ensure durable and effective window screen repairs. Fiberglass: Fiberglass screens are a popular choice due to their cost, flexibility, and resistance to corrosion. They provide excellent visibility and airflow while effectively keeping bugs out. Aluminum: Aluminum screens offer more strength than fiberglass. They are known for their sturdiness, making them a great option for areas with harsh weather conditions. Pet Screening: For pet owners seeking extra durability, we offer pet screening made from vinyl-coated polyester. This specialized screening is designed to withstand the playful antics of pets, including dogs and cats, providing added protection against scratches and tears.
